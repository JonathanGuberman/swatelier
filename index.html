About Swatelier

Swatelier generates 3D-printable flyswatter STL files with halftone image perforations. The design is inspired by Philippe Starck's Dr. Skud flyswatter, produced by Alessi.

Created by Jonathan Guberman using Cursor.

How to Use

  • Upload an image using the button or drag & drop onto the viewport.
  • Crop by clicking the image thumbnail. Drag to pan, scroll to zoom.
  • Adjust Density to control perforation detail and Invert to flip light/dark.
  • Open the Dimensions panel to adjust thickness, head size, handle length, and more.
  • Pick Mesh and Background colours to preview different looks.
  • Click Download STL to save the file for your slicer.

3D Viewport Controls

  • Rotate — click and drag
  • Pan — right-click drag, or Ctrl/⌘ + drag
  • Zoom — scroll wheel (zooms toward cursor)
